This service specification and these patient group directions and protocols are for the NHS Pharmacy First Service.

The NHS Pharmacy First Service incorporates the previous Community Pharmacist Consultation Service and builds on it to enable community pharmacy to complete episodes of care for 7 common conditions following specific clinical pathways.

This will enable the management of common infections by community pharmacies through offering self-care, safety-netting advice, and supplying certain over the counter and prescription only medicines via clinical protocol and patient group directions.

These files may not be suitable for users of assistive technology. Please contact ENGLAND.CommunityPharmacy@nhs.net to request an alternative format.

September 2025 update

Following a clinical stocktake, minor changes have been made to the NHS Pharmacy First Service clinical pathways, patient group directions (PGDs) and protocol. In anticipation of these changes coming into effect from Wednesday 1 October 2025, the updated service documents have been published. The current clinical pathways, PGDs and protocol will remain in place until 11.59pm on Tuesday 30 September 2025, and are available on the NHS Business Services Authority website.
